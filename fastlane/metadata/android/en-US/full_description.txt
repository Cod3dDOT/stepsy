<p><i>Stepsy</i> is a lightweight Android app for tracking your daily steps using your phone’s built-in sensors. A fork of <a href='https://github.com/0xf4b1/motionmate' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Motionmate</a>, it focuses on simplicity, battery efficiency, and full local data storage.</p><p>Stay active, monitor your progress, and reach your fitness goals — all through a clean, minimal interface with virtually no battery impact.</p><ul><li>Calculate distance and calories burned based on your height and weight</li><li>Visualize your data with dynamic, interactive charts</li><li>Export your data to a CSV file for backup and easy analysis</li><li>Includes a home screen widget to keep your daily steps visible at a glance</li></ul>
