<p><b>Stepsy</b> is perfect for anyone who wants a simple, private way to track daily steps without unnecessary bloat. It’s a lightweight, battery-friendly Android app that uses your phone’s built-in sensors. Forked from Motionmate, it prioritizes simplicity, privacy, and full offline data storage.</p><p>Stay active, monitor your progress, and hit your fitness goals — all through a clean, minimal interface with near-zero battery impact.</p><ul><li>Automatically calculate distance and calories burned based on your height and weight</li><li>View your activity trends with dynamic, interactive charts</li><li>Export your data as a CSV for backup or analysis</li><li>Use the home screen and lockscreen widgets to keep your step count front and center</li></ul>
